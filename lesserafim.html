LE SSERAFIM (르세라핌)

  • Le Sserafim is a South Korean girl group formed by Source Music.
  • The group consists of five members: Sakura, Kim Chae-won, Huh Yun-jin, Kazuha, and Hong Eun-chae.
  • LE SSERAFIM officially debuted on May 2, 2022 with their 1st mini album “FEARLESS”.
  • The name "LE SSERAFIM" is an anagram of the phrase "I'm Fearless"; it implies self-confidence and strong will to move forward without being swayed by the world's gaze.
